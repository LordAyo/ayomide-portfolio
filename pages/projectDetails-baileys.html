E-commerce Redesign

Baileys Boutique Redesign

A complete redesign of an e-commerce fashion boutique, focusing on improving the shopping experience, accessibility, and mobile responsiveness while maintaining the brand's elegant aesthetic.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Timeline

4 weeks

Tools

Figma

Design Process

01

Research & Analysis

Conducted user interviews, competitive analysis, and heuristic evaluation of the existing site to identify pain points and opportunities.

02

Information Architecture

Restructured the navigation and product categorization to improve findability and reduce cognitive load.

03

Visual Design

Created a modern, clean aesthetic that enhances product presentation while maintaining brand identity.

04

Usability Testing

Conducted multiple rounds of testing to validate design decisions and refine the user experience.
